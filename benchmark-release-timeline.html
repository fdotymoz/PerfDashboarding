On the dates. Years shown are the public release of each version. JetStream 2.1 was an interim benchmark-driver update with no widely-published release date — it is marked approx. and placed around 2020. MotionMark 1.0–1.2 predate the benchmark's move to open governance on GitHub; the dates shown are the original WebKit announcements, not the 2023 GitHub re-tag. The September 2027 markers are tentative next-generation releases (no version numbers announced yet) and are drawn as hollow, dashed points.
